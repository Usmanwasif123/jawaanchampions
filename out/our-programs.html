Our Programs

Competitions

Organizing regular sports competitions at various levels provides young athletes a platform to showcase their talents and develop their competitive spirit. These competitions foster teamwork, discipline, and a love for sports.

Teacher Training

We equip teachers with the knowledge and skills to implement school sports and leadership programs effectively. Our training sessions cover sports pedagogy, leadership development, and creating inclusive sports environments.

Specialized Kit Bags
(Specially designed for Modified Sports)

We ensure our participants have the necessary equipment to participate in sports by providing specialized kit bags filled with essential items such as bats, balls, and other sports gear. These kit bags help remove barriers to participation and make sports accessible to all

Modified sports

We are adapting popular sports to suit the resources available and the needs and abilities of young athletes with disabilities. Our modified sports programs promote inclusion, encourage participation, and provide opportunities for everyone to experience the joy of sports.

Physical Education Integration
in the existing curriculum

Working with schools to integrate physical education into the existing curriculum, ensuring that students have regular opportunities to engage in physical activity. This helps to promote a healthy lifestyle, improve academic performance, and develop essential life skills.
